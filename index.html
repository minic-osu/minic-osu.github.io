About Me

Hi! My name is Claudia Hilderbrand.

Redesigning interfaces is my passion. I love the challenge a pages content and layout brings and the possibilities it presents. 

My research focuses on creating gender-inclusive software in industry. I’ve made it my mission to help in the creation of usable and inclusive technology, making sure no group is left behind and everyone benefits equally from the advancements in technology. 

As a UX designer I've had the opportunity to teach UX concepts to companies in industry. In my portfolio you’ll find documentation, and reports I’ve written about UX techniques and designs.
